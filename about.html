Our Story

Born from a Love for Kashmir

KashmirHosts was founded in 2015 by Gazanfar Khan, a native of Kashmir who grew up witnessing tourists struggle with natural challenges, security concerns, and widespread rumors about the valley. These early experiences inspired him to create a reliable and authentic travel service that could offer safe, transparent, and memorable journeys across Kashmir.

After completing his studies, Gazanfar worked with multinational companies in Hyderabad and Bangalore. During this time, he was constantly approached by colleagues seeking guidance on visiting Kashmir. He also encountered many travelers who had been cheated by fraudulent operators and lacked proper local guidance.

"Driven by my hospitable nature and deep love for my homeland, I set out to transform how people experience Kashmir tourism. Starting with a single vehicle owned by my uncle, I built KashmirHosts on one belief: every guest is family."

— Gazanfar Khan, Founder & CEO

Our Values

What We Stand For

Honesty & Transparency

No hidden charges, no false promises. Complete transparency in pricing and itineraries — always.

Safety Above All

POSH-trained drivers, live GPS tracking, verified vehicles. Safety is non-negotiable — especially for ladies and solo travelers.

Responsible Tourism

We respect Kashmir's natural beauty, culture, and people. We actively promote eco-friendly travel and support local communities.

Our Commitment to Women Travelers

We Take Ladies' Safety Seriously

At KashmirHosts, we understand that safety is the number one concern for women traveling to Kashmir. That is why we invested early in POSH training for all staff, built live vehicle tracking, and created dedicated Ladies Escape packages with female guides on request.
