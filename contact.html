FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kashmir safe for ladies and solo female travelers?

Yes! KashmirHosts specifically invests in POSH-trained drivers and guides, live GPS tracking, female guide availability, and a dedicated 24/7 support line — making us the number one choice for solo female and ladies group travelers.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 2 to 3 weeks in advance for peak season (April to June, September to November). For Ladies Escape and Honeymoon packages, 1 month ahead is ideal to secure female guides and premium accommodation.

Do you provide airport pickup from Srinagar?

Yes! All packages include Srinagar Airport or Railway Station pickup. Our POSH-trained driver will be there with a name board. For ladies, we can arrange a female escort on request.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before. 50% refund for cancellations between 24 and 48 hours. Rescheduling is always free up to 72 hours before travel.

Can I customize a tour package?

Absolutely! All packages are fully customizable. WhatsApp us or fill the form above with your dates, group size, and preferences — we will create a personalized itinerary within hours.
